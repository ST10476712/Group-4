Empowering Workers, Cultivating Entrepreneurs

Empowering the Nation was established in 2022 and offers training courses in Johannesburg. We train domestic workers and gardeners on both six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes to make them more marketable.

Our Programmes:

Summary of Six-Month Courses (R1500 each)

  • First Aid (R1500)
    Purpose: To provide first aid awareness and basic life support.
  • Sewing (R1500)
    Purpose: To provide alterations and new garment tailoring services.
  • Landscaping (R1500)
    Purpose: To provide landscaping services for new and established gardens.
  • Life Skills (R1500)
    Purpose: To provide skills to navigate basic life necessities.

Summary of Six-Week Short Courses (R750 each)

  • Child Minding (R750)
    Purpose: To provide basic child and baby care.
  • Cooking (R750)
    Purpose: To prepare and cook nutritious family meals.
  • Garden Maintenance (R750)
    Purpose: To provide basic knowledge of watering, pruning and planting.
